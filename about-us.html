About Our Projects

RIDERE

RIDERE is a transportation app offering ride booking and parking services. Whether you need a ride or parking space, RIDERE has got you covered. It also provides transportation solutions for goods shifting.

99 STORES

99 STORES is an online grocery store providing fast delivery of daily groceries right to your doorstep. Shop from the comfort of your home and experience the convenience of quick grocery delivery.

School Management Software

The School Management Software offers easy access to individual student and staff analytics, including attendance, scores, and activities. Parents can track their child's progress instantly through this platform.

HRMS

HRMS is a comprehensive Human Resource Management System that manages everything from employee attendance and payslips to leaves and overtime. It provides detailed analytics for efficient HR management.

Matrimonial App

The Matrimonial App helps individuals find their partners by offering verified profiles and detailed information about users. If you're looking for a life partner, this app is your go-to solution.

Food Delivery App

The Food Delivery App offers fast and reliable food delivery services. Enjoy delicious meals delivered to your doorstep in no time.
